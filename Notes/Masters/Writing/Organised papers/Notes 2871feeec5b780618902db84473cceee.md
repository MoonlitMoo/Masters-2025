# General stats

- Redshift ~0.160
- Minihalo has angular scale ~30 arcsec (Y22) or ~80 pc maybe.BCG has scale <1.4 pc (G14), jets ~0.8 pc E-W (Y22)

# BCG

- Spectral index of ~0.87, and size <1.4 kpc (G14), 1.6 pc including arcs (Y22).
- Resolved jets shooting east and west, resolved to 0.8 pc.
- BCG has peak flux density 961 uJy/beam (Y22), 680 uJy/beam (H14). Potentially 40% variability over 8 years. Variability between 2020, 2022 < 20%, 955 uJy and 924 uJy respectively (Y22).
- Jets have flux density 801 uJy (Y22), prediction using G14 is 1.38 uJy. Accounting for variation of G14 measure in 1985 could be 60% in BCG flux, meaning full jet flux could have been measured in Y22, otherwise, some jet still not seen here.

# Head-tail radio galaxy

- Located NW of BCG, tail to south. Spectral index of ~1.05 (G14)

# Minihalo

- Emission is spiral and mostly constrained by the cold fronts of the cool core. Structure is ~80 kpc, extending out to ~230 kpc including spiral. (1)
